Fried Egg üç≥

Description

A fried egg is a simple yet satisfying dish made by cooking an egg in a pan with a small amount of oil or butter until the whites are firm, and the yolk remains runny or cooked to your preference. Rich in protein, healthy fats, and essential vitamins like vitamin D, B12, and choline, fried eggs are an excellent addition to a balanced diet.
They support muscle growth, brain function, and overall health. When paired with whole-grain toast or vegetables, fried eggs provide a nourishing, low-cost meal that can be enjoyed any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • Large Egg - 1
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Turn on the Stove and heat up the Pan.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of Extra virgin olive oil and 1 tbsp of Butter over medium heat. Stir until it melts.
  3. Crack 1 Egg into the pan carefully and try no to break the yolk.
  4. Tilt the pan and drizzle the butter on egg white carefully and not over the yolk.
  5. Season with Black pepper & Salt to taste.
